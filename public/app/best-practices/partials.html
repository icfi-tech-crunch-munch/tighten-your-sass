Partials & Importing

Sass allows you to import as many files as you wish with no performance hits due to render blocking or http requests. Take advantage of that to break up the site's CSS into small logical parts.

Sample folder structure:

/css
    /lib
        /plugin
            ...
            plugin.scss
    /sass
        _variables.scss
        _mixins.scss
        _utilities.scss
        _animations.scss
        _type.scss
        _grid.scss
        _forms.scss
        _tables.scss
        _panel.scss
        _buttons.scss

        styles.scss

styles.scss

@include '../lib/plugin/plugin';

@include 'variables';
@include 'mixins';
@include 'animations';

@include 'utilities';
@include 'grid';
@include 'type';

@include 'forms';
@include 'buttons';
@include 'tables';
@include 'panel';
